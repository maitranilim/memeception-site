Frequently Asked Questions

What is Memeception?
Memeception is a motion-first, real-time meme generator that instantly fetches the freshest content from Reddit's top humor communities. It uses a lightweight API to deliver memes without tracking or clutter.
Is this tool free to use?
Yes! Memeception is 100% free. You can browse unlimited memes, switch between dark and light modes, and save your favorites locally without paying a cent or seeing intrusive ads.
How do I save memes?
Simply tap the 'Floppy Disk' icon next to the 'Gimme Meme' button. Your saved memes are stored securely in your browser's local storage, meaning they persist even if you close the tab.
Why use this over Instagram?
Speed and focus. Memeception strips away the comments, likes, and algorithms, giving you raw, unadulterated humor in a clean, 3D interactive interface designed for pure enjoyment.
